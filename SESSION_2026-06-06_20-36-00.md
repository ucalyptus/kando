## pytest-bdd 8.x gotcha (discovered last session, still relevant)

Step definitions are **per-module** — steps in `world_steps.py` are NOT visible in
`world_spec_steps.py`. Solution: re-declare shared steps directly in each step file
(with `_` prefix by convention). Never rely on cross-file step sharing.

## mutmut 3.x gotcha (discovered this session)

`also_copy` must include everything the test sandbox needs to import:
- `kits/` — needed by `test_diligence_kit.py`
- `features/` — needed by BDD step files (`scenarios("../001-world/spec.md")`)

Without these, mutmut's sandbox fails at collection time.
